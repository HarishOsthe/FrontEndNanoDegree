Pikachu

Pikachu one of the most well-known varieties of Pokémon, largely because Pikachu is a central character in the Pokémon anime series. Pikachu is regarded as a major character of the Pokémon franchise as well as its mascot, and has become an icon of Japanese pop culture in recent years. It is also seen as one of the major mascots for Nintendo.

  • Pokémon Type: Electric (Mouse Pokémon)
  • Length: 1' 4"
  • Weight: 13 lbs.
  • HP: 50

Spark If your opponent has any Benched Pokémon, choose 1 of them and this attack does 10 damage to it.
